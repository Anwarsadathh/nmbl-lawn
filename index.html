NMBPL Stadium · Rajajipuram

At NMBPL Stadium Lawn & Banquet, every celebration is designed to feel special, elegant, and effortless. With large open lawns, refined banquet facilities, and complete event support, we offer an ideal venue for hosting memorable gatherings of every scale.

Whether you are planning an intimate family function or a large social event, our venue combines big space, flexibility, and professional management to create an experience your guests will remember for years.

Located in the heart of Rajajipuram, NMBPL Stadium Lawn & Banquet provides easy accessibility along with a premium event environment that suits weddings, receptions, corporate gatherings, and private celebrations alike. We offer in-house catering with wide range of menu options, music system on request, and elegant lighting and decoration. There are both indoor and outdoor event venue options available with experienced team of seamless event execution.
